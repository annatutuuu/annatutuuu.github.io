## Data Analysis — ADHD & Environmental Risk Factors

- Conducted EDA on ADHD datasets using **R Studio**, encompassing 3000+ raw data points across 7 variables including ADHD Odds Ratio, Metal Exposure, and Year.
- Cleaned and preprocessed data by merging multiple datasets, handling missing values, extracting numeric variables from string fields via regex, and converting categorical variables into quantitative formats.
- Built **simple and multiple linear regression models** to examine the impact of Metal Exposure and Soil Concentration Rate on children's ADHD risk; improved model R² from 0.007 → 0.20 by introducing soil concentration as a second predictor.
- Performed temporal trend analysis across three datasets (Metals, Plasticizers, Organic Contaminants), identifying conflicting directional trends and concluding the year-OR relationship is statistically inconclusive (p > 0.05).
